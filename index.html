Introduction

As a person born after 2000, I find myself not quite into those modern sci-fi, AI styles stuff. I'm always more into those old rough arts of human hands. As I see the soul of any types of art is thoughts, thoughts inspired by what we saw and how did we felt.

That's a strong power of emotion in those thoughts, which I think, is the value a man can have, a value that's so unique and can't be repalced by any thoughtless forces.
